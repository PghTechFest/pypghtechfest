Keynote

Jennifer Davis, Software Engineer at Chef

Jennifer is the co-author of Effective DevOps. She is a co-organizer of devopsdays Silicon Valley, founder of Coffeeops. She supports a number of community meetups in the San Francisco area. In her role at Chef, Jennifer develops Chef cookbooks to simplify building and managing infrastructure. She has spoken at a number of industry conferences about devops, tech culture, monitoring, and automation. When she’s not working, she enjoys hiking Bay Area trails, learning to make things and quality time with her dog, George.
